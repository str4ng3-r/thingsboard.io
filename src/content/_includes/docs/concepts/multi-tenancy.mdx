**Why multi-tenancy?** Running one platform instance for many business units or customers cuts infrastructure costs and centralizes administration, without sacrificing data isolation or security.

**ThingsBoard organizational hierarchy:**

- **System Administrator:** Platform administrator who creates and manages tenants, and controls system-wide resources without owning IoT entities.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/multi-tenancy' target="_blank">Tenant:</DocLink> Top-level organizational entity representing an independent company or business unit. Owns and manages its resources, such as devices, assets, and dashboards.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/customers' target="_blank">Customer:</DocLink> Logical sub-organization within a tenant, representing a client organization, regional branch, or internal department — used to organize and scope a subset of the tenant's resources for secure access delegation.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/users' target="_blank">User:</DocLink> Authenticated account with assigned <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/roles' target="_blank">roles and permissions</DocLink>. Can belong to either the tenant or one of its customers and accesses only the resources available within its scope.

**How are tenants isolated?** Isolation is enforced at the platform level: each entity belongs to exactly one tenant, and permissions are governed by <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/roles' target="_blank">role-based access control</DocLink>. A Tenant Administrator can never see or access another tenant's resources, while the System Administrator retains global visibility across all tenants.

**What is multi-tenancy on ThingsBoard Cloud?** ThingsBoard Cloud is a shared multi-tenant platform. Every <a href="/pricing/?product=thingsboard-cloud" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Cloud subscription</strong></a> is a **Tenant** — your own isolated environment, owning its devices, assets, and dashboards. You get full data isolation without operating any infrastructure yourself.

**How does ThingsBoard Cloud organize tenants?** ThingsBoard provisions and manages your tenant for you — each subscription maps to exactly one Tenant. The account created during subscription becomes that tenant's **Tenant Administrator**, with full administrative access to its resources and configuration — scoped entirely to this tenant, with no visibility into other tenants or platform-wide administration.
