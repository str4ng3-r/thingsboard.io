Defines who owns what and how users access the system.

- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/multi-tenancy'>System:</DocLink> Global entity managing platform settings and tenants. Controls system-wide resources without owning specific IoT entities.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/multi-tenancy'>Tenant:</DocLink> Top-level organizational entity representing company or business unit. Owns and manages scoped resources: **Devices**, **Assets**, and **Dashboards**.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/customers'>Customer:</DocLink> Logical sub-organization or separate entity managed by a Tenant. Used to isolate resources for secure data sharing, like **client organizations**, **regional branches**, or **internal departments**.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/users'>Users:</DocLink> Authenticated account with permissions. Can belong to either a Tenant or a Customer.


<Aside type="tip">
Use **Customers** to securely delegate access. A Customer User can only interact with entities specifically assigned to their Customer account.
</Aside>
