Defines incoming telemetry processing and workflows.

- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/system/'>System:</DocLink> Global entity managing platform settings and tenants. Controls system-wide resources without owning specific IoT entities.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/tenants/'>Tenant:</DocLink> Top-level organizational entity representing company or business unit. Owns and manages scoped resources (devices, assets, dashboards) and defines primary security boundaries.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/customer/'>Customer:</DocLink> Logical sub-organization within Tenant. Used to isolate devices, assets, and dashboards for secure data sharing and delegated management.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/ui/users/'>Users:</DocLink> Authenticated account with permissions. Can belong to either a Tenant or a Customer.
