An **alias** is a reference to a single entity or group of entities used as a data source in widgets. Aliases can be **static** (pointing to fixed entities) or **dynamic** (resolved at runtime based on entity type, relations, or dashboard state).

<DocImage src="/src/assets/images/user-guide/aliases/alias-scheme.png" alt="Alias architecture" width="80%" />

## Prerequisites

Before proceeding, follow the <DocLink product={props.product} path="getting-started-guides/helloworld">Getting Started guide</DocLink> to become familiar with ThingsBoard devices, dashboards, and widgets.

## Creating an alias

<Steps>
1. Open your dashboard and enter **Edit mode**.
2. Click the **Entity aliases** icon in the upper-right corner of the dashboard.
3. In the opened dialog, click **Add alias**.
4. Enter a name for the alias, select a **Filter type**, fill in the required fields, and click **Add**.
5. Click **Save** in the lower-right corner to apply.
6. To use the alias, click the **pencil** icon on a widget, navigate to the **Entity alias** tab, select the alias from the dropdown, and click **Add**.
</Steps>
