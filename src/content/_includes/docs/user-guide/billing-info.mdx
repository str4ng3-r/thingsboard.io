The **Plan and billing** section lets tenant administrators manage subscriptions, monitor usage, configure billing details, and review invoices directly from the ThingsBoard Cloud UI.

All subscription management, payment processing, and card storage are handled entirely by [Stripe](https://stripe.com/). ThingsBoard does not store your credit card data.


## Accessing Plan and billing

Navigate to **Plan and billing** from the main menu. Only **tenant administrators** have access.

When action is required — for example, if billing information is incomplete — a warning banner appears at the top of the page.

The section contains up to five tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **<DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/billing-info/subscription'>Subscription</DocLink>** | View your current plan, usage limits, and update your subscription |
| **<DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/billing-info/billing-details'>Billing information</DocLink>** | Configure payment method and billing address |
| **<DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/billing-info/invoices'>Invoices</DocLink>** | View past invoices and preview upcoming charges |
| **Audit logs** | Review history of billing-related actions |

<Aside type="note">
The **Billing information**, **Invoices**, **Upcoming invoice**, and **Audit logs** tabs appear only after you upgrade from the Free plan. On the Free plan, only the **Subscription** tab is visible.
</Aside>

## Audit logs

The Audit logs tab shows a history of billing-related actions — plan changes, payment method updates, coupon applications, and other administrative events. Use audit logs for troubleshooting and compliance tracking.
