The **Billing information** tab in <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/billing-info'>Plan and billing</DocLink> displays two side-by-side cards: **Payment method** and **Billing information**. This tab is available only on paid plans — see the <DocLink product={props.product} path='user-guide/billing-info'>overview</DocLink> for details.

<Aside type="note">
All payment data is processed and stored exclusively by [Stripe](https://stripe.com/). ThingsBoard never has access to your full card details.
</Aside>

## Payment method

Shows your current card on file, or **NO PAYMENT METHOD PROVIDED** if none is set.

<Steps>
1. Click **Add** (or **Update**) to open the Stripe-powered payment dialog.
2. Enter your **Card Holder Name**, **Country**, and **Credit or debit card number** (including expiry MM/YY and CVC).
3. Accepted cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
4. Click **Add** to store the payment method.
</Steps>

## Billing information

Shows your billing email and address, or **NO BILLING ADDRESS PROVIDED** if not yet configured.

<Steps>
1. Click **Update** to open the billing information dialog.
2. Fill in: **Company**, **Email**, **Phone** (E.164 format, e.g. +12015550123), and billing address fields — **Country**, **City**, **State/Province**, **Zip / Postal Code**, **Address**, **Address 2**.
3. Click **Update**.
</Steps>
