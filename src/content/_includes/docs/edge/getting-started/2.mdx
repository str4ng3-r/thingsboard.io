In this step, you will create a device on Edge and activate it.

---

## Step 2.1 Add a device on Edge

Edge synchronizes devices to your ThingsBoard account automatically.

<Steps>
1. In the Edge UI (<a href="http://localhost:8080" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">http://localhost:8080</a>), go to **Entities → Devices** and click **Add device** (+).

2. Enter a name and click **Add**.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
The device is automatically synchronized to your ThingsBoard account. You can verify this by going to **Entities → Devices** in your ThingsBoard account.
</Aside>

---

## Step 2.2 Activate the device

After you create the device, a **Check connectivity** dialog appears with a pre-configured command for your selected protocol and operating system.

<Steps>
1. In the **Check connectivity** dialog, select your preferred protocol and run the displayed command. A `200 OK` response confirms Edge received the data. The device status changes to **Active**.

2. **Optional:** In your ThingsBoard account, go to **Entities → Devices** to confirm the device appears there.
</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
If you closed the dialog, reopen it by clicking the device and then clicking **Check connectivity**.
</Aside>
